🌿 FocusBridge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

FocusBridge ("the App") is developed by SFL Consulting, operated by Stéphane François Lefort, based in Herstal, Belgium. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we handle.

1. Data We Collect

Location data (optional): If you grant permission, we use your device's GPS coordinates solely to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times for your location via the Aladhan API (api.aladhan.com). We do not store, transmit, or share your location with any third party. If you decline location access, prayer times are calculated using approximate local methods.

App usage data: Your progress (XP, completed quests, collected verses, prayer tracking, garden state) is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's localStorage or the device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Personal information: The optional name you enter during onboarding is stored only on your device. We have no access to it.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

— Email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
— Analytics, tracking, or behavioral data
— Advertising identifiers or marketing profiles
— Health data beyond the self-reported prayer and quest tracking you choose to use
— Any data from children under 13 (see section 5)

3. Third-Party Services

Aladhan API: We send your GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude only) to api.aladhan.com to retrieve prayer times. Aladhan's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data. No personal identifiers are sent.

Google Fonts: The app loads the Outfit and Amiri fonts from Google Fonts. Google's standard font serving privacy policy applies.

We use no analytics services, no advertising networks, and no social media trackers.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We operate no servers and maintain no databases containing user information. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all information is permanently deleted. We cannot recover it.

5. Children's Privacy

FocusBridge includes a "Kids mode" designed for users aged 7-14. This mode simplifies language and adjusts the interface. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The optional name field in Kids mode is stored exclusively on the device and is never transmitted.

Parents and guardians: the app does not contain advertising, in-app purchases, social features, or any mechanism for children to share personal information.

6. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

As a Belgian company, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we store no personal data on our servers, most GDPR provisions regarding data access, rectification, and deletion are handled automatically — your data exists only on your device and under your control. For any GDPR-related inquiries, contact us below.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time using the "Reset Data" button in the app's Insights tab, or by uninstalling the app. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
